What is Zikre Hussain?

Zikre Hussain Is An Online Marshiya Player App..


This App is designed for Dawoodi Bohra's. With this app you can Listen n download audio of Marasiya, Madeh, Rasa of Syedna Mohammed Burhanuddin, Nasihat, Qasida, Salaam, Iltija, Manaqabat, Naat....

It contains approximately 1160 Titles, 1264 Audio files
